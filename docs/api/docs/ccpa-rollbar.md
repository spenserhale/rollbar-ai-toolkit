# Rollbar & CCPA

<p>Rollbar values your privacy and we have implemented the steps to be in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act. Below provides additional specific information for consumers based in California as required by law:</p>
<h3>Collection and Use of Personal Information</h3>
<p>In the last 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information.</p>
<ul>
<li>Identifiers, such as your name, email address, telephone number or other similar identifiers </li>
<li>California Customer Records (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.80(e)), such as username and password, company name, job title, and business email address </li>
<li>Geolocation data, such as information about your location (at country and city level) collected from your IP address </li>
<li>Other personal information, such as personal information you provide to us in relation to a survey, comment, question, request, or inquiry and any other information you upload to our application </li>
<li>Internet/network information, such as the browser type, IP address, unique device identifiers, language preference, referring site, the date and time of access, operating system, and mobile network information. Your devices (depending on their settings) may also transmit location information to the Services. </li>
</ul>
<p>We collect personal information directly from you, from your browser or device when you visit our websites, from third parties that you permit to share your information or from third parties that share public information about you.</p>
<p>See the section, “Purposes for Collection” of our Privacy Policy to understand how we use the personal information collected from California consumers.</p>
<h3>Recipients of Personal Information</h3>
<p>We share personal information with third parties for business purposes. The categories of third parties to whom we disclose your personal information may include: (i) our service providers and advisors, (ii) marketing and strategic partners; (iii) ad networks and advertising partners; and (iv) analytics providers.</p>
<p>Please see the “Information Sharing and disclosure” section of the Privacy Policy for more information.</p>
<h3>California Privacy Rights</h3>
<p>As a California resident, you may be able to exercise the following rights in relation to the personal information about you that we have collected (subject to certain limitations at law):</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>The right to know</strong> any information relating to your personal information that we have collected and disclosed in the last 12 months (upon verification of your identity) </li>
<li><strong>The right to deletion</strong> of personal information we have collected from you </li>
<li><strong>The right to opt-out</strong> of personal information sales to third parties now or in the future. However, <strong>we do not sell</strong> your personal information </li>
<li>You also have the right to be free of discrimination for exercising these rights. </li>
</ul>
<p>Please note that if exercising these rights limits our ability to process personal information (such as a deletion request), we may no longer be able to provide you with our products and services or engage with you in the same manner.</p>
<h3>How to Exercise Your California Consumer Rights</h3>
<p>To exercise your right to know and/or your right to deletion, please submit a request at <a href="https://rollbar.com/contact/?inquiryType=Other">Contact Us</a> or by email at <strong>privacy@rollbar.com.</strong></p>

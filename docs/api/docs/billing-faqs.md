# Billing FAQs

## How do I send invoices to my finance team?

Your finance or accounting teammates do not need a Rollbar account to get copies of your Rollbar invoices emailed to them. Note, they will not get any project data or alerts. Here is how you sign up those users to get invoices sent their way:

* Click your user icon in the bottom left to open the menu
* Click on `Your Plan and Billing`

![](https://files.readme.io/292af0d-image.png)

* Click on the `Payment Details` button in the `Subscription` section

![](https://files.readme.io/8ebf7b6-image.png)

* This will bring you to your payment details page.
* Simply add the email(s) of people you want to receive invoices.
  * To enter multiple emails, separate them with a comma for example: `testingemail@example.edu, testingemail2@example.edu`.
* Click the `Save` button when you have finished adding details.

![](https://files.readme.io/0c536ea-image.png)

## How do I add additional details to my invoices?

You can add additional details to your invoices by adding this data to the `Extra Billing Info` section to your invoices. This can be whatever data you require on your invoices, such as a VAT number or address. This extra billing information is automatically applied to any future invoices. If you need this information added to your past invoices, then please contact Rollbar's support team [here](https://rollbar.com/contact/).

Here is how you can add this additional details to your invoices:

* Click your user icon in the bottom left to open the menu
* Click on `Your Plan and Billing`

![](https://files.readme.io/292af0d-image.png)

* Click on the `Payment Details` button in the `Subscription` section

![](https://files.readme.io/8ebf7b6-image.png)

* This will bring you to your payment details page.
* Simply add the data you need in the `Extra Billing Info` section.
* Click the `Save` button when you have finished adding details.

![](https://files.readme.io/0c536ea-image.png)

## How do I find my invoices?

Rollbar makes it simple to view and retrieve your current & past invoices. The invoices include any previous bills & payment receipts. Any of your Rollbar members in your `Owners` team would be able to access your invoices. To do this they can:

* Click your user icon in the bottom left to open the menu
* Click on `Your Plan and Billing`

![](https://files.readme.io/292af0d-image.png)

* This will navigate you to your plan settings.
* Click on the `Invoices` button in the `Subscription` section.

![](https://files.readme.io/7f498a2-image.png)

* This takes you to your list of invoices to review.
* If you want to look at a specific invoice, click on the `View` button next in the invoice.

![](https://files.readme.io/7433248-image.png)

* You can download your invoice as a PDF or HTML file by clicking the ` Download as PDF`  or `Download as HTML` buttons near the bottom of the page.
* Additionally, you can download your Usage Detail Reports by clicking on the links at the bottom of the page.

## Credit Billing

**Why was I charged immediately when I added Credit?**

Adding or increasing Credit is treated as an upgrade. Like all upgrades in Rollbar, it takes effect immediately, and you are billed pro-rated for the remainder of the current period.

**Why does my Credit balance reset each month?**

Credits are a monthly subscription add-on, not a permanent balance. They expire at the end of each billing cycle to keep costs predictable.

**Can I prevent unexpected Credit usage?**

Yes — set a Spend Budget in **Account Settings → Billing Info → Credit Budget** to cap monthly credit spend. You can also monitor your current balance from the Credit widget visible on related feature pages.

**What happens if I run out of credits mid-task?**

Rollbar will complete the in-flight run even if your balance hits zero. Your balance may go negative as a result. If you have on-demand enabled, the overage is billed as on-demand instead.

**Why can't I add Credit on the Free (Basic) plan?**

Credit purchasing is only available for Essentials and Advanced plans initially.

**Does the annual plan discount apply to Credits?**

No. Credits are billed monthly and are excluded from the annual plan discount.

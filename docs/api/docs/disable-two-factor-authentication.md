# Disable Two Factor Authentication

<p>If you no longer have access to your 2FA device and do not have your recovery codes, Rollbar cannot disable 2FA on your user account unless you are on a paid account (see below).</p>
<p>There are a couple of possible solutions that can allow you to regain access to your account.</p>
<ul>
<li>
If there is another user in your account that is a member of the Owners team, they can disable 2FA for you via the Account Settings --&gt; Members screen.
</li>
<li>
If you are the only Owner in your account, and your 2FA device was a smart phone (e.g. iPhone, Android), then you can restore a backup of your device that has your authentication app properly configured.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Note: If you are a paying customer, we can make two small charges (less than $1 US) to your credit card. If you can tell us the amount of those charges, then we will disable 2FA and immediately reverse the charges.</p>

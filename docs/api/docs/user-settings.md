# User Settings

Manage your Rollbar user account settings, account/team membership, and connections to other accounts

The [User Settings](https://rollbar.com/settings/profile/) page can be found by clicking your username/avatar and 'Manage Settings' in the lower-left corner of the Rollbar UI. You'll also find direct links to Authentication Options, Email Settings, and Connected Accounts in the resultant menu.

## General

On the [General](https://rollbar.com/settings/profile/) page, you're able to change your Avatar, Username, and Team membership. You're also able to delete your user.

## Authentication Options

On the [Authentication Options](https://rollbar.com/settings/authentication/) page, you're able to change your password, enable/manage [Two Factor Authentication](https://docs.rollbar.com/docs/two-factor-authentication), and manage Github/Google OAuth login options.

## Email Settings

On the [Email Settings](https://rollbar.com/settings/email/) page, you're able to add additional email addresses, as well as manage which is your primary email address. In addition, you can choose which accounts and projects you'd like to receive notifications for.

## Connected Accounts

On the [Connected Accounts](https://rollbar.com/settings/integrations/) page, you're able to manage your Rollbar user's connections to user accounts on other services, such as Github, Shortcut, and JIRA. Many Rollbar integrations require or benefit from pairing your account across these services.
